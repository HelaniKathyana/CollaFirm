Leader in the development of Porcine Based Collagen Products


CollaFirm, LLC is a medical device company focused on research and product development. We develop porcine based collagen technologies and products for implants and surgical applications. We are working with leading corporations in the field as their outsourced research team. Our vision is that the market for collagen-based products from porcine will grow rapidly over the next few years.


CollaFirm, LLC is founded by Surendra Batra, Ph.D. Dr. Surendra Batra has over 20 years of experience researching with collagen and has been integral in bringing many collagen-based medical products to market for different leading entities. Dr. Batra is considered to be a leading collagen expert. During this time, he has focused on multiple collagen-based medical applications.
